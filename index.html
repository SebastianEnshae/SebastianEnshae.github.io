Hey, I am Sebastian, but let me share my story from New Jersey all the way out to California, where it just so happens that I am having an exciting adventure with my little brother Adrian. I grew up in New Jersey, and everything about my childhood surrounded me with a melting pot: fast-moving cultures, great history, and a balance of urban and suburban life. As fond as I was of my hometown, I always had this West Coast attraction with its endless sunshine combined with its innovative spirit and laid-back lifestyle. I made a bold move and decided to relocate to California. It wasn't just about switching snow for sun; this felt like an amazing opportunity to completely redo my life and deeply explore my passions. California's pulsating vibe, the creativity oozing out of every nook and cranny, just led me to dream big. I wanted to dive into something that would not only resonate with my interests but also contribute positively. That is the point when I joined forces with Adrian, who had always shared my interest in fashion and creativity. With his fresh energy and my experience coming together, we brainstormed ways to translate a shared passion into business. That's how our vintage clothing resale venture was born. Fashion is one of the strongest tools for self-expression, and through selling vintage clothes, we make others find their style while making sure Earth isn't harmed. Adrian and I started our business scouring the many thrift shops, flea markets, and estate sales throughout California. Every time we go out, it's like a treasure hunt: sifting through hundreds of items to find just one buried treasure. We're especially attracted to items with history-ones that have been loved and worn previously. Each of the items we find has a story to be told, and we seek to connect these stories with new owners who love their character and charm. We soon came to realise that it wasn't actually just about the clothes we were selling, but our journey connected us with our community and really shared our love for vintage. We launched an online store and started to host pop-up events where we could showcase our finds, meet other like-minded people, and share tips on sustainable fashion. Our business has also allowed us to work with local artists and designers on one-of-a-kind pieces that capture the true California eclectic style. It is a creative synergy that has enriched not only our brand but also our appreciation for the vibrant culture surrounding us. It has most definitely been the support from customers that rounds out this journey so nicely. Listening to stories about why they love vintage and how they are styling our pieces brings us much joy. We have created a community of vintage lovers who share in our values for creativity and sustainability, and we look forward to continuing to grow that community. Looking into the future, Adrian and I have big plans regarding our company: to increase our online presence, move into new markets, and probably even open a physical store in the near future. But above all, we would like to continue spreading our idea of fashion being stylish, yet sustainable.
